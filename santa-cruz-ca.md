# Detailed Notes

## Lulu Carpenter's

Cozy historic-building café on Pacific Ave; espresso, breakfast/brunch, weekend live jazz.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Lulu+Carpenter%27s+1545+Pacific+Ave+Santa+Cruz+CA)

## 11th Hour Coffee

Local roaster known for clean, intentional single-origin coffee in an industrial-modern space.

- Good lighting
- Mixed crowd

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=11th+Hour+Coffee+1001+Center+St+Santa+Cruz+CA)

## Verve Coffee Roasters (Pleasure Point)

Verve's original roastery/café; surf-culture vibe, acclaimed specialty roaster. 

- This location is a little busy and touristy, limited seating.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Verve+Coffee+Roasters+816+41st+Ave+Santa+Cruz+CA)

## UC Santa Cruz Arboretum & Botanic Garden

Sprawling botanic garden on the UCSC campus with rare plant collections, especially proteas, banksias, and other Southern Hemisphere species.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=UC+Santa+Cruz+Arboretum+%26+Botanic+Garden+Santa+Cruz+CA)

## DeLaveaga Disc Golf Course

Famous, hilly, wooded disc golf course; birthplace of several pro disc golf events and considered a classic, challenging layout.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=DeLaveaga+Disc+Golf+Course+Santa+Cruz+CA)

## Natural Bridges State Park

State park with tide pools, a monarch butterfly natural preserve (seasonal), and the namesake natural rock bridge/arch at the beach.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Natural+Bridges+State+Park+Santa+Cruz+CA)

## Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk

Classic oceanfront amusement park with the historic Giant Dipper wooden roller coaster and arcade games.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Santa+Cruz+Beach+Boardwalk+Santa+Cruz+CA)

## Seabright Deli

Neighborhood deli in the Seabright area; sandwiches and casual lunch spot.

- Long lines!
- The Cove sandwich = very good
- The Sailor sandwich (pastrami) = good

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Seabright+Deli+Santa+Cruz+CA)

## Seabright Beach

Wide, sandy beach near the harbor; popular for sunbathing and a calmer, more local alternative to the Boardwalk beach.

- For picnic, can grab a sandwich from Seabright Deli

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Seabright+Beach+Santa+Cruz+CA)

## Bad Animal

Bookstore-bar-restaurant hybrid downtown; browse books while having dinner or drinks.

- Great ambiance, but a little pricey
- Good wines
- Pork chop = excellent
- Chicken Snitzel = ok

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Bad+Animal+1011+Cedar+St+Santa+Cruz+CA)
