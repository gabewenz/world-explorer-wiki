- Great German beer on draft
- Brats are solid

## Tasty Pizza and Indian Subs

Surprisingly good Indian pizza.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Tasty+Pizza+and+Indian+Subs+Santa+Clara)

## Falafel Stop

Recommend the falafel pita with extra tahini.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Falafel+Stop+Sunnyvale)

## 7 Leaves

Great mung bean milk tea.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=7+Leaves+Cupertino+Village)

## Pacific Pinball Museum

Interactive museum with 100+ playable pinball machines; admission includes unlimited play.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Pacific+Pinball+Museum+Alameda) · [Website](https://www.pacificpinball.org/)

## Pulgas Water Temple

Small Greco-Roman style temple and reflecting pool off I-280 (via Edgewood Rd to Cañada Rd), built in 1934 to celebrate completion of the Hetch Hetchy aqueduct. Pedestrian access 9am-3:30pm daily.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Pulgas+Water+Temple+Redwood+City) · [Website](https://www.sfpuc.gov/learning/come-visit/pulgas-water-temple)

## June's Pizza

Wood-fired pies in an unmarked West Oakland warehouse; tiny menu (margherita, pepperoni, rotating seasonal), famous for burying every pizza in a blizzard of fresh-grated parmesan. Whole pies only until 9pm (~$35), then $5–6 slices until midnight — check Instagram for nightly specials.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=June%27s+Pizza+West+Oakland)

## The Marshall Store

Oyster bar/general store on Tomales Bay; good day trip for fresh oysters.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=The+Marshall+Store+Marshall+CA)
