# Detailed Notes

## AYCE GOGI

- All-you-can-eat Korean BBQ in Van Nuys
- Great selection of pinball machines (make sure to check out the backroom!)

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=AYCE+GOGI+Van+Nuys+CA)

## Blue Note Hollywood

Jazz club in Hollywood.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Blue+Note+Hollywood+CA)

## Bridge to Nowhere

- Popular hike along the East Fork of the San Gabriel River, ending at a 1930s arch bridge that was never connected to a road. 
- Several river crossings involved.
- Use caution on hot days, bring enough water.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Bridge+to+Nowhere+hike+Azusa+CA)

## Caltech

Walk the campus of Caltech (California Institute of Technology).

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Caltech+Pasadena+CA)

## Colorado Street Bridge

Historic 1913 concrete arch bridge over the Arroyo Seco with scenic views.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Colorado+Street+Bridge+Pasadena+CA)

## EightyTwo LA

Bar and arcade in DTLA.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=EightyTwo+LA+DTLA)

## Fork in the Road

Giant 18-foot wooden fork sculpture at the spot where Pasadena Avenue splits — a quirky local landmark.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Fork+in+the+Road+Pasadena+CA)

## Geoffrey's Malibu

Upscale oceanfront restaurant on PCH with ocean views. Food is solid (not great), but the
views are incredible and it draws a quirky LA crowd. Reservations recommmended.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Geoffrey%27s+Malibu+CA)

## Getty Villa

Museum of ancient Greek, Roman, and Etruscan antiquities in a villa setting on PCH. Timed-entry tickets required (free).

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Getty+Villa+Pacific+Palisades+CA)

## Griffith Observatory

Iconic art deco observatory in Griffith Park with panoramic views of LA and the Hollywood Sign. Grounds are free; planetarium shows cost extra.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Griffith+Observatory+Los+Angeles+CA)

## Hollywood Bowl

Iconic outdoor amphitheater in the Hollywood Hills. Great for summer concerts; bring a picnic.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Hollywood+Bowl+Los+Angeles+CA)

## Huntington Gardens

World-class botanical gardens, art galleries (including Gainsborough's Blue Boy), and old books.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=The+Huntington+San+Marino+CA)

## Jumbo's Clown Room

Iconic dive bar and burlesque venue in Hollywood.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Jumbo%27s+Clown+Room+Hollywood+CA)

## Maru Espresso Bar

Fancy espresso bar. Long line, very little seating, no bathroom. But it has good drinks
and great people (and dog) watching.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Maru+Espresso+Bar+Beverly+Hills+CA)

## Mulholland Drive

Scenic road through the Hollywood Hills and Santa Monica Mountains with sweeping views of LA and the valley.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Mulholland+Drive+Los+Angeles+CA)

## Point Dume

Coastal headland in Malibu with hiking, ocean views, and tide pools. Classic Malibu.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Point+Dume+Malibu+CA)

## Porto's Bakery

Beloved Cuban bakery. Famous for croquetas, potato balls, and pastries. Long lines, good value.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Porto%27s+Bakery+Burbank+CA)

## Rodeo Drive

Famous luxury shopping street in Beverly Hills.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Rodeo+Drive+Beverly+Hills+CA)

## Santa Monica Boardwalk

The Santa Monica Pier and beachfront boardwalk, with Pacific Park amusement rides and street performers.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Santa+Monica+Pier+CA)

## Seven Grand

Whiskey bar in DTLA.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Seven+Grand+DTLA+Los+Angeles+CA)

## Space Shuttle Endeavour

Space Shuttle Endeavour on display at the California Science Center in Exposition Park.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=California+Science+Center+Los+Angeles+CA)

## Sugarfish

Popular sushi chain with high-quality set menus for a reasonable price.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Sugarfish+Los+Angeles+CA)

## Will Geer Theatricum Botanicum

- Charming professional outdoor theater in Topanga Canyon, with lots of Shakespeare works.
- Parking is along the canyon road, which can be annoying.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Will+Geer+Theatricum+Botanicum+Topanga+CA)

## Zankou Chicken

- Armenian Lebanese rotisserie chicken chain; multiple LA locations
- Avoid the Hollywood location
- Famous for the garlic paste
- Beef shawarma and chicken tarna plates are great

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Zankou+Chicken+Los+Angeles+CA)

## Zuma Beach

Wide sandy beach in Malibu, one of LA's best for swimming. Can have epic sunsets.

[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Zuma+Beach+Malibu+CA)
